# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We — the maintainers and contributors of this project — pledge to make participation
a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability,
ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are not professional software developers. We built this to run our own family office,
we use it every day, and we share it openly because we'd genuinely love the community's
help to make it better. Contributions, questions, and feedback are all welcome.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, public or
private harassment, publishing others' private information, and other conduct which could
reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to
the maintainers at **bubbleinvest.ai@gmail.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and
investigated promptly and fairly.
